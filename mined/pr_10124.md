AUTHOR: ajr-khll

## BODY
Issue #9310 reported issues with consistent monitor selection when opening new windows.

I found that the lastCascadePoint in TerminalController.newWindow was always cascading from 0,0 which seems to always be the primary monitor.

I made a small adjustment to the function to compute the parent's cascade point, add the offset and then cascade the new window from that point. In testing this has new windows always open on the same monitor as the currently focused window, rather than always on the primary monitor.

I was unable to replicate the tab-related problem in the original issue.

I used OpenAI's Codex to assist in understanding the codebase, but made edits manually.
